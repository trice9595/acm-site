Software as a Service

Industry based devlopment group for students.

Purpose

To give students hands-on experience designing, developing, and releasing software using the principles practiced by major enterprises.

What We Do

Support personal and project development

The Software as a Service Group is here to provide guidance and resources to self-motivated students who want to further their education outside of the classroom. This group is about connecting like-minded students that want to work together to make something great. If you've ever had an exciting idea for a project but didn't have the time or resources, come to one of our meetings.
