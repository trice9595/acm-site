Software as a Service

Industry based devlopment group for students.

Purpose

To give students experience designing, developing, and releasing software through the practices of major enterprises.

What we do

Support personal development and projects

The Software as a Service Club is here to provide guidance and resources to self-motivated students who want to work with others to make something great. If you've ever had an exciting idea for a project but didn't have the time or resources, come to one of our meetings.

Connect students

First and foremost, this club is about connecting like-minded students. Students from all majors are welcome and will likely be necessary to see a project through.
